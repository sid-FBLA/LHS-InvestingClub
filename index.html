About Us

Welcome to Investing Club at Livingston High School! We’re a fully student-run organization dedicated to teaching high school students basic information about finance and investing. From working the Stock Market to managing personal finances, Investing Club touches on numerous important topics throughout the year. Our members are provided with an unmatched business experience as we host events such as Investing competitions and games. Not only that, but members have the ability to work together to become better investors through sharing their experiences and advice with each other. Finally, our members have access to presentations from business professionals and guest speakers with countless years of experience.
